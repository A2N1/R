Projects in R

Level 1: Beginner (Data Cleaning & Basics)
- iris: Floral Classification. Basics of scatter plots and grouping.
- palmerpenguins: Exploratory Analysis. Handle missing data (NA) and create boxplots.

Level 2: Intermediate (Statistics & Relationships)
- mtcars: Linear Regression. Predict fuel efficiency (mpg) based on car weight.
- diamonds: Pricing Models. Use facet_wrap to see how "Cut" affects price across 54k rows

Level 3: Advanced (Data Architecture)
- nycflights13: Relational Data. Join 5 tables (flights, weather, planes) to find delay causes.
- gapminder: Global Trends. Recreate Hans Rosling’s bubble chart of Life Expectancy vs. GDP.
- USArrests: Cluster Analysis. Group US states into "crime profiles" using K-means.

Level 4: Expert (Specialized Analytics)
- AirPassengers: Time Series. Predict future growth by decomposing trends and seasonality.
- janeaustenr: Text Mining. Sentiment analysis to track the "emotional arc" of a novel.
- maps: Geospatial Mapping. Create a heat map (Choropleth) of US crime rates by state.
